Terms: Refunds

Refunds are available -- minus $150 administrative fees -- up until 30 calendar days before the training begins. If you require a refund, please email the accounting department.

Terms: Transfers

MerbClass registrations are transferable up until 10 calendar days before the training begins. If you would like to arrange a transfer, please email us your information, and an email address for the individual taking your place. We will then send them a specific registration link.
